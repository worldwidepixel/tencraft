This is PridePack. A LGBTQ+ Pride themed Resource Pack made by Pridecraft Studios. We make Minecraft queer. We also have compatibility with some mods, such as Paladin's Furniture Mod.

Who

We're Pridecraft Studios: A team that makes LGBT content for Minecraft. We're working on PridePack, PrideMod, and a few other projects.

How can I test new stuff out?

You can join our Discord or follow our Mastodon and get early features sneak-peaks, or check out the GitHub Releases for pre-releases. We'll post early unreleased content, such as early alpha PrideMod releases on our Ko-Fi.

What mods are currently supported?

Currently, the only themed mod is Paladin's Furniture Mod, which is available on the base Pridepack resource pack. We plan adding support for the Create Mod, More Axolotl Variants Mod, Botania and many other mods we get suggestions for. If you have an idea, send it In this channel on our discord.
